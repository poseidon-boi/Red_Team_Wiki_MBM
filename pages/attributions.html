Varun

Student

Tasks:

Dry Lab, Research, Wet Lab

Specific tasks:

1. Pepper foot Rot Gave project proposal for the synbio solution of foot rot in black pepper after encountering problems with treating root rot in turmeric. Conducted an in-depth impact analysis of pepper foot rot, including references for research papers. Researched current strategies for the treatment of foot rot in pepper and other plants, identified past iGEM teams, and designed novel approaches for our project. Worked on synbio solution approaches - using microbes as biocontrol agents and proposed a mediated antifungal release in the presence of an effector released by P. capsici. Effector selection: RXLR(242) and corresponding receptors, NLPs and corresponding receptors, Ellicitins (INF1) - retrieved and searched for NCBI, PDB, UniProt entries. Helped characterize key target points in the infection cycle and secretion locations of different effector proteins. Worked on siRNA interference of key genes for pathogen virulence and survival. Used SignalP and characterized signal peptides in the ELR protein sequence. Used TMHMM and InterPro to identify and characterize specific domains in ELR. Worked on a two-component system for the binding and signaling pathways for mediated antifungal release.
2. Iron Toxicity Conducted impact analysis for iron toxicity in rice plants.
3. Lignin Proposed lignin extraction instead of the previous project focused on lignin degradation. Brainstormed lignin biosynthesis and discussed threshold limits for different enzymes in the biosynthesis pathway.
4. Avian Influenza Virus Pitched the idea for on-site HPAI detection. Conducted an in-depth impact analysis for the project. Coordinated three IHP meetings with Archana Ma'am. Participated in an HP meeting with the Director of Serum Institute, Pune. Studied the infection cycle of the virus and the structure of different viral components. Identified key targets for detection - viral RNA sequences and membrane proteins (HA, N). Conducted extensive research on current detection strategies and identified gaps for improvement and novelty in our project. Researched the Toehold switch-based detection method and specificity. Researched CRISPR-based detection methods and their limitations - specifically their dependence on RNA enrichment before CRISPR-based detection. Proposed a novel combination of nanoparticle-based RNA enrichment strategies before CRISPR-based detection. Worked on a lateral flow-based detection system incorporating HA/N binding peptides and proteins.
5. Soil Salinity Pitched the idea for coastal soil desalination. Conducted general impact analysis for the problem statement, SDGs, and stakeholders. Researched existing methods. Proposed multiple synbio solutions for the problem statement, including enhancing plant tolerance to high salt concentrations, sequestering, and crystallization.
